The opportunity

The real issue is not only moving furniture. It is visibility, proof and trust.

When customers do not know what is happening, small delays can become reputation problems. This demo shows how a moving company can create a clearer, more accountable customer experience.

⌁

Unclear updates

Customers need simple move status updates instead of repeated calls and WhatsApp follow-ups.

▣

Inventory uncertainty

Photo-backed item records reduce disputes about what was loaded, delivered or reported missing.

✓

Weak claims handling

A structured claim flow helps management review damage or missing item reports with context.
