Our Services

Comprehensive Radiology Solutions

From equipment procurement to radiation safety — we provide end-to-end support for imaging facilities.

X-Ray Equipment

New and pre-owned radiographic systems, portable X-Ray units, DR retrofit panels, and C-arm solutions from leading manufacturers.

Radiation Protection

Lead aprons, thyroid shields, protective eyewear, lead gloves, radiation badges, shielding barriers, and mobile shields.

CR & DR Imaging

Computed radiography readers, cassettes, phosphor plates, and direct digital retrofit kits for transitioning from analog to digital.

PACS & IT Solutions

Picture archiving and communication systems, DICOM viewers, RIS integration, archive servers, and imaging workflow software.

Accessories & Supplies

X-Ray markers, positioning sponges, calibration phantoms, film/screen systems, contrast media injectors, and room consumables.

Service & Repair

On-site and depot repair services for X-Ray tubes, generators, tables, buckys, DR detectors, and CR readers. Fast turnaround.
